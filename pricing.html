Frequently Asked Questions
Can I change plans later?
Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is my health data secure?
Absolutely. All data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM at rest and in transit. We maintain HIPAA-compliant audit logging and never sell or share your health data with third parties.
What counts as a "patient"?
A patient is any person you're tracking health information for -- your mother, father, spouse, child, or even yourself. Each patient gets their own profile with separate medical records.
How does caregiver sharing work?
With the Family plan, you can invite other family members to view and manage patient profiles. Each caregiver gets their own login, and all actions are logged for accountability.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. We offer a full 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us and we'll process your refund immediately.
